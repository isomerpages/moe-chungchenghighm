**今年的5月30日，我们诚挚邀请学生、家长、校友和各位公众来中正总校，一同观赏原创舞台剧《湖的传奇之湖光长照中正人》。**

本剧由中正校友、文化奖得主韩劳达先生创作，并由制作团队改编，亦有学生参与创作。作品以中正的发展历程为背景，通过戏剧形式串联起不同年代的故事与记忆，呈现一段跨越时光的集体回望。

本次演出在学校管理委员会的大力支持下呈现，是竹林楼大礼堂重启的重要里程碑之一。制作自去年年底启动筹备，由中正中学（总校）与中正中学（义顺）的学生联合参演，并融合多元表演艺术元素，包括两校的表演艺术团体及校友共同参与，为观众呈现一场丰富而多层次的舞台体验。

*   日场：下午2时30分至4时

*   地点：中正中学（总校）大礼堂
    
*   &nbsp;票价：$30 / $25 / $20

凡购票观赏演出的观众，亦可参与当天下午4时30分至7时30分于湖畔长廊举行的“中正艺术节”。现场设有多元活动摊位与美食摊车，让您可以在轻松氛围中享用小吃、参与手作体验，并欣赏由中正中学（总校）及南洋初级学院舞蹈团带来的精彩表演。

**We warmly invite students, parents, alumni and all to come to Chung Cheng and experience our original stage production, Lake Odyssey 2026: Echoes by the Lake.**

Written by Chung Cheng alumnus and Cultural Medallion recipient Mr Han Laoda, and adapted by the production team with contributions from our students, this production weaves together stories and memories from different generations of the Chung Cheng community.

Presented with the strong support of the School Management Committee, this milestone production has been in preparation since late last year. It features a joint cast of students from Chung Cheng High School (Main) and Chung Cheng High School (Yishun), and brings together a rich integration of the arts, including performances by our performing arts groups and alumni.

**Performance Details**

*   Date: Saturday, 30 May 2026
    
*   Matinee: 2.30 pm – 4.30 pm
    
*   Evening: 7.30 pm – 9.30 pm
    
*   Venue: Grand Auditorium, Chung Cheng High School (Main)
    
*   Ticket Prices: $30 / $25 / $20

All ticket holders are also invited to the Festival Interlude, held at the concourse by the lake from 4.30 pm to 7.30 pm on the same day. Enjoy a variety of activity booths and food carts, take part in hands-on craft activities, and catch lively performances by the dance groups from Chung Cheng High School (Main) and Nanyang Junior College.
