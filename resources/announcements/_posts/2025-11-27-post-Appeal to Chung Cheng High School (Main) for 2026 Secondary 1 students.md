For students who would like to appeal for transfer to CCHM, you will need to submit an online appeal form.

The link is as follows:[https://go.gov.sg/cchm25-s1-appeal](https://go.gov.sg/cchm25-s1-appeal)

The deadline for the submission of appeals is on 28 December 2025, 2359h. The application is deemed to be unsuccessful if you do not hear from the school by 9 January 2026.

Thank you.
