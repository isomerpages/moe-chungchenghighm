    <td class="tg-u1cn">Language and Cultural Enrichment 语言与文化增益课程</td>
    <td class="tg-u1cn">Secondary 1 and 2 低年级</td>
    <td class="tg-u1cn">Students will learn Chinese calligraphy on a fortnightly basis and attend weekly Appreciation of Chinese Culture lessons. These lessons introduce students to key aspects of Chinese heritage, including the history of our forefathers and selected works from Chinese literature, helping them develop a deeper understanding and appreciation of Chinese culture.
<br><br><span style="font-weight:400;color:#1E1E1E">Beyond the classroom curriculum, the programme provides authentic learning experiences through partnerships with external organisations. Students will visit the Singapore Chinese Cultural Centre, where they may serve as student docents or participate in curated cultural workshops. They will also embark on learning journeys to watch local Chinese theatre productions, deepening their appreciation of Chinese performing arts.</span>
<br><br><span style="font-weight:400;color:#1E1E1E">In addition, the Special Development Programme is offered to students who demonstrate strong interest and ability in areas such as public speaking and creative writing. Through the programme, students will have opportunities to further develop their talents, take part in enrichment activities, and represent the school on relevant platforms and competitions.</span>
<br><br><span style="font-weight:400;color:#1E1E1E">学生通过双周一次的书法课和每周一节的中华文化与历史课程，浸润于文化学习。课程内容涵盖先辈南来的历史以及文学作品的导读，帮助学生加深对中华文化的理解与欣赏。
在课堂学习之外，课程也通过与对外的合作，为学生提供真实且丰富的学习体验。学生将参访新加坡华族文化中心，在那里担任学生讲解员或参与精心设计的文化工作坊。此外，学生也将通过学习之旅观赏本地华文戏剧演出，从而提升对表演艺术的欣赏能力。
此外，学校也为在口语表达与创意写作方面展现浓厚兴趣与潜能的学生提供特别发展课程。通过该课程，学生将有机会进一步发展自身的才华，参与多元化的增益活动，并在相关平台或比赛中代表学校展示所学。</span></td>
