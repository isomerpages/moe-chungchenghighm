-------

In line with our vision of building generations of leaders who are grounded in values and global in outlook, our CCE curriculum seeks to develop students through platforms that inspire their thinking and experiences that touches their hearts. In turn, we hope that our students embody the school values and attributes of the Chung Cheng learner to become a leader with passion, integrity, humility and compassion.

The CCE curriculum is taught through our Values and Leadership Education (VLE) lessons every Tuesday. Students also develop their character through National Education (NE) events, Values in Action programmes (VIA), Co-curricular Activities (CCA) and daily interactions with their teachers and peers.

We seek to develop the following outcomes in our lower secondary students.  

**Lower Secondary Outcomes**

*   **Managing Self**
*   **Relating well with others**
*   **Serving the immediate community**
*   **Understanding the nation**
