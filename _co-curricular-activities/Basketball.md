Our Basketball Team emphasises on teamwork and resilience in training our players. Perseverance is the key, as our players constantly seeks new challenges beyond the National Schools Games to better themselves and play to their fullest potential. Our basketball players strive to compete with sportsmanship, as our teachers and coaches believe in character building through sports.
