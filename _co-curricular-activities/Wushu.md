Our Wushu Team boasts a strong tradition for the love and respect for Chinese Martial Arts. Our teams strive to put in their best in the National Schools Games, competing for the medals as a united team. Both our teachers and coaches are committed to guide and inspire our students to improve their performances and teamwork in court.
